Our Vision for Water

Creating a sustainable water future

Water is a precious resource, yet across Kenya and beyond, significant amounts are lost daily due to inefficient leak reporting and management. This widespread issue leads to increased costs, damaged infrastructure, and unnecessary water scarcity.

At AquaTrack, we envision a future where every drop counts and where communities and water utility providers are empowered with the tools to effectively combat water loss.

What is AquaTrack?

Innovative leak detection platform

AquaTrack is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize how water leaks are reported and managed. Our system consists of both a user-friendly mobile app and a comprehensive web dashboard for water companies.

We're moving beyond slow phone calls and emails, enabling real-time communication and rapid response to leakage incidents. By integrating cutting-edge AI for image recognition and geolocation tracking, AquaTrack ensures that reported leaks are verified quickly and prioritized effectively.
